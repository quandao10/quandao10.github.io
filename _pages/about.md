I am 3rd year PhD student at Rutgers University under supervision of <a href='https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=a7VNhCIAAAAJ&hl=en'>Distinguished Prof. Dimitris Metaxas</a>. My research focuses on generative models, specifically diffusion models and autoregressive models, with a primary emphasis on fundamental research. For diffusion models, I concentrate on developing efficient and robust training methodologies. During my PhD, I was very lucky to do internship in Apple MLR. Previously, I was a Research Resident under the supervision of <a href='https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=FYZ5ODQAAAAJ&hl=en'>Dr. Tuan Anh Tran</a> at <a href='https://www.vinai.io/'>QualcommAI Research, Vietnam</a> (which was VinAI research) and spent 2 wonderful years there. I received a bachelor degree in computer science from Monash University in 2020.
